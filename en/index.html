Docking steps

1. Business application

We signed relevant cooperation agreements with Yolanda commercial staff and provided independent 'appId' and 'configuration file' after our review

2. Download SDK-Demo

Obtain the SDK-Demo running experience of the corresponding platform. The Demo displays the SDK usage logic for reference.

Suggest developers 'star * our SDK project, timely get SDK related update notification.

  1. Design device functions Design device usage functions on existing apps

General equipment use steps are as follows:

  1. Add devices

    This is not paired on Android or iOS. Bluetooth 4.0 supports non-paired use. Our equipment belongs to a custom protocol, which can independently and accurately identify a certain device type.

  2. Measurement process

    The measurement process includes Bluetooth scanning and connection, data interaction and other processes. At the same time, users are given certain status display, so as to check the device status in real time.

  3. Save and display data

    After receiving the measurement data, save the data locally and display it. To improve user experience, you are advised to save the data locally and then upload it to the access cloud center. If the data cannot be uploaded to the access cloud center in time, you can also upload it to the access cloud center at a fixed time. You can refer to our APP for data display, or design the UI style by yourself.

  4. Develop APP functions According to the design function, APP development. Pay special attention to the location permission and location service switch of different Android operating system versions. For iOS, it is 13 and requires Bluetooth permission.
